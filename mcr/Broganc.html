Brogan Cemetery

Near Brogan, Oregon

1983

The Brogan Cemetery is located about 3 miles from Highway 26. There is a dirt road leading to this beautiful well kept cemetery. The grounds are enclosed with a fence. The grass is well maintained. There is a total of 42 graves. Fourteen graves are unmarked or the markers are illegible.

T 15S, R 42E, S 26. Records compiled by: Barbara McCormick and her sister Pat.
