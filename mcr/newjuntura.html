New Juntura Cemetery

Juntura, Oregon

1983

T 21S, R 38E, S 17

This cemetery is located on Highway 20. The cemetery is enclosed with a fence. It is overgrown with grass and greasewood. The grounds are very poorly kept. There is no road leading to the cemetery, only a cow path. One grave is located 100 ft. west of the rest of the graves. It is a duplicate of a marker found in the cemetery. This was to replace the original one which started to crumble. Records compiled by Grant and Sybil Higley.
