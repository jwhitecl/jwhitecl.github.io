Watson Cemetery

Watson Oregon no longer exists. The Owyhee reservoir now covers the town of Watson. The cemetery is up on a hill above where the town used to be. It is accessible only by boat. The cemetery is located on the south side of Owyhee reservoir. Records from this cemetery were compiled by Grant and Sybil Higley, with help from the Malheur County Sheriffs Department.
