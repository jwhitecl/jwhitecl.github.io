SUCCOR CREEK MC BRIDE CREEK ROAD CEMETERY 1983 T 25 S R 46E   This cemetery is located off Highway 95, 1.9 miles west of the Idaho Oregon boarder. It is located in a fenced pasture of about 2 acres with only one stone remaining upright, the animals have knocked over all the rest. There were a total five stones with ten inscriptions. We were able to stand the ROSE family stone in an upright position. Records compiled by Grant and Sybil Higley.
