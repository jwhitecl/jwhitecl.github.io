HARPER CEMETERY

Harper, Malheur County, Oregon

The old Harper Cemetery is on the hill overlooking Harper. It is known to have had five graves there at one time. One of the graves has been moved away. All of the facts that are available about this old burial place are given here. T 19S, R 43E, S 29. Records compiled by Grant and Sybil Higley
