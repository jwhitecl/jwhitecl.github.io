Malheur City Cemetery

Malheur City (now a ghost town)

November, 1983

Malheur Cemetery is located on a dry hill overlooking the site of old Malheur City, which is now a ghost town. The cemetery is fenced and some small trees and shrubs are growing around the inside of the enclosure. They are kept alive by the water that flows from a pipe that has been driven into the hillside at the highest corner of the cemetery. Every year about a week before Memorial weekend many citizens gather from the surrounding communities to clean up the cemetery. Records compiled by Grant and Sybil Higley.

There were some records that were recently sent to my from Wendell Kochis, President of the Malheur City Cemetery.
