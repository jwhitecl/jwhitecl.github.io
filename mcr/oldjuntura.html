OLD CEMETERY

Juntura, Oregon

1983

(R38E T21S)

This cemetery is located an Highway 20, 1/4 mile west of town. The cemetery is fenced in and located in the middle of a rancher's field. It is overgrown with greasewood. Cows have broken the fence and knocked over some headstones. Some of the graves are unmarked and obliterated by animals. Records compiled by Grant and Sybil Higley.
