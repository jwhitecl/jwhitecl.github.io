Ironside Cemetery

Ironside, Oregon

November, 1983

This beautiful little cemetery is located high on a hill over looking Ironside, Oregon. The cemetery is enclosed with a fence, and the landscape includes grass, pine trees, and shrubs on one side. The cemetery is well cared for. It is located right next to Highway #26 and can be seen from the highway. Records compiled by Barbara McCormick and her sister Pat.
